READING







NO LONGER HUMAN
Osamu Dazai



" Mine has been life of much shame "

" The one thing I must avoid is becoming offensive
in their eyes; I shall be nothing, the wind, the sky "











Reading had always been a passion of mine ever since I was a child
Among the vast array of literary genres, classic literature stands as my undisputed favorite
It is within the pages of classic literature that I discover the essence of humanity—the struggles,
triumphs, and enduring questions that transcend the confines of any era. The elegant prose, intricate
storytelling, and profound insights found in classic literature have shaped my appreciation for the
art of language.Through my love for classic literature, I have witnessed the evolution of ideas
and the indelible impact that great works of fiction can have on society. These novels, often penned
centuries ago, continue to resonate with readers around the globe, providing timeless lessons,
stirring emotions, and profound contemplation.
